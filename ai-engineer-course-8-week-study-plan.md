## Tips for the Accelerated Pace

1. **Leverage your experience:** You already know Python, LangChain, RAG, and LLM concepts from the Ed Donner course. Speed through familiar material at 1.5–2x playback speed.
2. **Week 8 flexibility:** The final week packs three modules. Speech Recognition and AI Ethics are largely conceptual — you can watch these at higher speed. LLM Engineering is more hands-on, so take your time there.
3. **Priority sections:** If pressed for time, prioritize RAG (Section 42), LCEL (Section 41), LangGraph (Sections 45–47), and the LLM Engineering prototype (Section 66).
4. **Hands-on practice:** Even at an accelerated pace, code along with the exercises — passive watching won't stick.
5. **Consider a 9-week variant:** If Week 8 feels too heavy, split it: Week 8 = Speech Recognition (Sections 52–61), Week 9 = LLM Engineering + Ethics (Sections 62–77).
